Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 May 2026  ·  Operated by Siam T. Paite

LifeHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use LifeHub:

  • Account information — name, email address, and authentication credentials when you register.
  • Profile data — information you voluntarily add such as subscriptions, warranties, skills, and savings records.
  • Payment information — transaction hashes for crypto payments and order references for Gumroad purchases. We do not store full payment card details.
  • Device data — browser type, device identifiers, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the LifeHub service.
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • To send push notifications for expiring warranties and upcoming subscription renewals.
  • To process and verify payments and grant plan entitlements.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication, database storage, and push notifications.
  • Gumroad — for processing subscription payments (governed by Gumroad's Privacy Policy).
  • Anthropic — anonymised warranty receipt data may be processed by Claude AI for OCR extraction.
  • Vercel — for hosting and serving the application.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at siam_t_paite@lifehub.fit.

5. User Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email siam_t_paite@lifehub.fit with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion.

6. Cookies and Tracking

LifeHub uses essential session cookies required for authentication. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, Firebase security rules, and encrypted data storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

LifeHub is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us at siam_t_paite@lifehub.fit to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
