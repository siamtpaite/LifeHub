What is LifeHub?

LifeHub is a progressive web app (PWA) that acts as a central hub for managing the practical parts of your life. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, reminder apps, and receipts, LifeHub brings everything into one intelligent, always-accessible dashboard.

It works entirely in your browser — no app store required — and can be installed on your phone or desktop just like a native app.

Getting Started

LifeHub takes less than 2 minutes to set up. Here's how:

1

Create your account

Visit lifehub.fit and sign in with Google, Facebook, or your email address. No credit card required.

2

Allow notifications

When prompted, click Allow to enable push notifications. This is how LifeHub alerts you about expiring warranties and upcoming renewals.

3

Add your first item

Head to Subscriptions or Warranty Vault and tap the Add button. For warranties, you can upload a receipt photo and let AI fill in the details.

4

Install as a PWA (optional)

On Chrome/Edge: click the install icon in the address bar. On iOS Safari: tap Share → Add to Home Screen. LifeHub works offline once installed.

📦 Warranty Vault

The Warranty Vault helps you keep track of every product warranty you own. Never lose a receipt or miss a claim window again.

Adding a warranty

1

Tap "+ Add warranty"

Opens the add form with two options: AI scan or manual entry.

2

Upload your receipt

Take a photo or upload an image. Claude AI reads the receipt and auto-fills the product name, purchase date, and price.

3

Set expiry date

Enter the warranty period (e.g. 2 years from purchase). LifeHub calculates the expiry and will alert you 5 days before.

ℹ️ If AI extraction fails or the receipt is unclear, you can always fall back to manual entry — just fill in the fields yourself.

💳 Subscriptions

Track every recurring charge — streaming services, SaaS tools, gym memberships, everything. LifeHub shows your monthly and annual totals so you always know what you're spending.

What you can track

For each subscription, you can log: service name, amount, billing cycle (monthly/yearly), next billing date, category, and notes. LifeHub automatically calculates your total recurring spend.

⚠️ Set your billing currency in the top-left dropdown to see all amounts converted correctly.

🧠 Skills Exchange

The Skills section lets you build a personal skills profile and connect with others in your network. Think of it as a mini LinkedIn, but focused purely on skill trading.

How it works

Add skills you have (e.g. "Python programming", "UI/UX Design"). Browse skills others have offered. Propose a trade — you teach them X, they teach you Y. Use SkillsChat to coordinate.

💬 SkillsChat (Pro) gives you access to 6 real-time chat channels organised by skill category for connecting with other LifeHub users.

💰 Savings Goals

Define financial targets and track your progress toward them. Whether you're saving for a trip, a gadget, or an emergency fund — LifeHub keeps you accountable.

Each goal has a name, target amount, current amount, deadline, and optional notes. The progress bar updates in real-time as you log contributions.

🤖 AI Advisor Pro

The AI Advisor is powered by Claude (Anthropic) and has full context of your LifeHub data. Ask it anything:

"Which of my subscriptions am I probably not using?"
"What warranties are expiring in the next 30 days?"
"How much am I spending on entertainment subscriptions per year?"
"Suggest ways to cut my monthly recurring costs by 20%."

The AI Advisor is available to Pro subscribers only. Each conversation is private and not used to train AI models.

🔔 Smart Alerts

LifeHub sends push notifications directly to your browser or phone — no email required. You'll receive alerts when:

  • A warranty is expiring within 5 days
  • A subscription renewal is due within 5 days

Alerts are sent daily at 8:00 AM IST. To receive them, make sure you've allowed notifications when prompted on first login.

Re-enabling notifications

If you accidentally blocked notifications, go to your browser settings → Site permissions → Notifications → find lifehub.fit → Allow.

📊 Analytics

The Analytics panel gives you a visual overview of your financial and lifestyle data. It includes a subscription spend breakdown by category (donut chart), month-by-month spending trends (bar chart), and predictive forecasts for the next 3 months.

Advanced predictions and forecasting are available on the Pro plan.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is LifeHub really free?
Yes — the free tier is free forever. You get subscriptions tracking (up to 5), warranty vault (up to 3), skills, savings goals, push notifications, and basic analytics with no time limit.
Does LifeHub work on mobile?
Yes. LifeHub is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On iOS, open it in Safari and tap Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, tap the install prompt in Chrome. It works just like a native app.
Is my data secure?
All data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) with strict security rules. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What happens if I buy on Gumroad with a different email?
The auto-upgrade only works if you use the same email as your LifeHub account. If you used a different email, contact us at siam_t_paite@lifehub.fit and we'll manually upgrade your account.
Can I get a refund?
All payments are final per our Terms of Service. However, if you encounter a technical issue preventing access to Pro features, contact us and we'll resolve it promptly.
How does AI receipt scanning work?
We use Claude Vision (Anthropic) to read your uploaded receipt image and extract product name, purchase date, and price. The image is processed server-side and not stored permanently.
How do I delete my account?
Email us at siam_t_paite@lifehub.fit with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We'll delete all your data within 30 days.
Which crypto networks are supported?
We support native SOL on Solana, native ETH on Base (L2), and native TRX on Tron. We do not support ERC-20 tokens, BEP-20 tokens, or other wrapped assets — only the native coins of each chain.
I have a feature request or found a bug.
We'd love to hear from you. Email siam_t_paite@lifehub.fit with details. We read every message.
